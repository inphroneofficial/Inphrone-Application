## Company Incorporation

### Recommended Structure: Private Limited Company

**Why Private Limited?**
- ✅ Limited liability protection
- ✅ Easier to raise investment
- ✅ Credibility with B2B clients
- ✅ Tax benefits for startups
- ✅ Required for Startup India registration

### Step-by-Step Process

#### Step 1: Obtain Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
- **What**: Electronic signature for online filings
- **Where**: Authorized DSC agencies (eMudhra, Sify, nCode)
- **Documents**: PAN Card, Aadhaar, Passport photo
- **Cost**: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500
- **Time**: 1-2 days

#### Step 2: Director Identification Number (DIN)
- **What**: Unique ID for company directors
- **Where**: MCA Portal (www.mca.gov.in)
- **Documents**: PAN, Aadhaar, Address proof
- **Cost**: ₹500 (government fee)
- **Time**: 3-5 days

#### Step 3: Name Approval (RUN - Reserve Unique Name)
- **What**: Reserve company name with MCA
- **Names to Reserve**: 
  - INPHRONE TECHNOLOGIES PRIVATE LIMITED
  - INPHRONE ENTERTAINMENT INTELLIGENCE PRIVATE LIMITED
  - INPHRONE INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED
- **Cost**: ₹1,000 (government fee)
- **Time**: 2-3 days

#### Step 4: SPICe+ Form Submission
- **What**: Single form for incorporation, PAN, TAN, GST
- **Documents Required**:
  - MOA (Memorandum of Association)
  - AOA (Articles of Association)
  - Registered office address proof
  - Director consent letters
  - Subscriber details
- **Cost**: ₹7,000 - ₹10,000 (government fees)
- **Time**: 5-7 days

#### Step 5: Certificate of Incorporation
- **What**: Official document confirming company formation
- **Includes**: Company Identification Number (CIN)
- **Time**: Same day as SPICe+ approval

### Registered Office Requirements (Hyderabad)

**Documents Needed**:
- Rent agreement (if rented) OR ownership proof
- NOC from property owner
- Utility bill (electricity/water) not older than 2 months
- Address: Full address with pin code

**Note**: Can use residential address initially

## Trademark Registration

### Trademarks to Register

| Trademark | Class | Description |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| **INPHRONE** (Word Mark) | Class 35 | Advertising, market research, data analytics |
| **INPHRONE** (Word Mark) | Class 42 | Software, SaaS, technology services |
| **INPHRONE Logo** | Class 35 & 42 | Visual brand identity |
| **"People-Powered Entertainment Intelligence"** | Class 35 | Tagline/slogan |
| **InphroSync** | Class 42 | Feature name |
| **Your Turn** | Class 42 | Feature name |

### Registration Process

#### Step 1: Trademark Search
- **Where**: IP India website (ipindia.gov.in)
- **Purpose**: Check for similar existing marks
- **Cost**: Free (online) or ₹500-1,000 (professional search)

#### Step 2: File Application (TM-A Form)
- **Where**: IP India portal
- **Cost**: ₹4,500 per class (online, for individuals/startups)
- **Documents**:
  - Logo in JPG format (if logo mark)
  - Signed Form TM-48 (if using agent)
  - Proof of use (if already in use)

#### Step 3: Examination
- **Time**: 6-12 months
- **Response**: May receive objections to address

#### Step 4: Publication in Trademark Journal
- **Time**: After examination clearance
- **Opposition Period**: 4 months

#### Step 5: Registration Certificate
- **Time**: 18-24 months total
- **Validity**: 10 years (renewable)

### Important Notes
- Apply under **"Proposed to be Used"** if not yet launched commercially
- Use ™ symbol immediately after application
- Use ® symbol only after registration

## Patent Filing

### Patentable Aspects of Inphrone

| Innovation | Patent Type | Description |
|------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Audience Intelligence Algorithm** | Utility Patent | Method for aggregating and analyzing entertainment preferences |
| **Gamification Engine** | Utility Patent | System for incentivizing user participation |
| **Real-time Insights Generation** | Utility Patent | Process for converting opinions to actionable insights |
| **Multi-stakeholder Platform** | Utility Patent | Architecture connecting audiences with creators |

### Patent Application Process

#### Step 1: Provisional Application
- **Purpose**: Secure filing date while finalizing invention
- **Cost**: ₹1,600 (for startups)
- **Time**: 1-2 weeks to prepare
- **Validity**: 12 months (must file complete within this period)

#### Step 2: Complete Specification
- **Within**: 12 months of provisional
- **Cost**: ₹4,000 (for startups)
- **Includes**: Detailed description, claims, drawings

#### Step 3: Publication
- **Time**: 18 months from filing (or early publication on request)
- **Cost**: ₹2,500 (for early publication)

#### Step 4: Examination Request
- **Within**: 48 months of filing
- **Cost**: ₹4,000 (for startups)

#### Step 5: Examination & Grant
- **Time**: 1-3 years after examination request
- **Responses**: Address any objections from examiner

### Patent Filing Tips
- File **provisional patent first** to secure date
- Document everything with dates (invention notebooks)
- Consider **international filing (PCT)** for global protection
- Startup India registered companies get **80% fee rebate**

## Required Documents Checklist

- [ ] PAN Card (self-attested copy)
- [ ] Aadhaar Card (self-attested copy)
- [ ] Passport (if available)
- [ ] Passport size photographs (5)
- [ ] Bank statement or utility bill (address proof)
- [ ] Mobile number and email ID
- [ ] Educational qualification proof

#### Company Documents
- [ ] Proposed company names (3 options)
- [ ] Business objectives description
- [ ] Registered office address proof
- [ ] NOC from property owner
- [ ] Rent agreement (if applicable)
- [ ] Utility bill of registered office

### For Trademark Registration

- [ ] Logo in high resolution (300 DPI, JPG/PNG)
- [ ] List of goods/services description
- [ ] Applicant's details (name, address, nationality)
- [ ] Power of Attorney (if using agent)
- [ ] Proof of prior use (if applicable)

### For Patent Filing

- [ ] Invention description (detailed)
- [ ] Technical specifications
- [ ] Drawings/flowcharts
- [ ] Claims statement
- [ ] Abstract (150 words max)
- [ ] Inventor details and assignment deed

- [ ] Incorporation certificate
- [ ] PAN of company
- [ ] Brief description of business
- [ ] Explanation of innovation
- [ ] Declaration of not being formed by splitting/restructuring
