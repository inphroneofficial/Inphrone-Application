### Sales & Revenue Department

#### Sales Team Structure (At Scale)

```
VP Sales
├── Enterprise Sales (50+ employees)
│   ├── Sr. Account Executive (x4)
│   └── Sales Engineer (x2)
│
├── SMB Sales (5-50 employees)
│   ├── Account Executive (x4)
│   └── SDR (x4)
│
├── Partnerships
│   └── Partnership Manager (x2)
│
└── Revenue Operations
    ├── Sales Ops Analyst
    └── CRM Admin
```

#### Sales Process
1. **Lead Generation** (SDRs + Marketing)
2. **Qualification** (SDRs)
3. **Discovery Call** (AE)
4. **Product Demo** (AE + Sales Engineer)
5. **Proposal** (AE)
6. **Negotiation** (AE + VP Sales)
7. **Close** (AE)
8. **Handoff** (AE → Customer Success)
