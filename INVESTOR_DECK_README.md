## ðŸ“Š Key Metrics & KPIs to Track

### North Star Metric
**Weekly Active Opinions (WAO)**: Total opinions submitted per week across all categories

### User Metrics
- **User Growth Rate**: % increase in registered users month-over-month
- **Weekly Active Users (WAU)**: Users who submit at least 1 opinion/week
- **Retention Rate**: % of users active 30/60/90 days after signup
- **Opinion Completion Rate**: % of users who submit opinions weekly
- **Average Opinions per User**: Total opinions / active users

### Revenue Metrics
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Subscription revenue per month
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: MRR x 12
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Marketing spend / new paid users
- **Lifetime Value (LTV)**: Average revenue per user over their lifetime
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: Target >3:1 for healthy unit economics
- **Churn Rate**: % of paid users canceling monthly
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)**: MRR / total paid users

### Engagement Metrics
- **Time on Platform**: Average session duration
- **Opinions per Session**: How many opinions users share per visit
- **Upvote Rate**: % of opinions receiving upvotes
- **Feature Adoption**: % of users engaging with analytics dashboards
- **Referral Rate**: % of users inviting friends

### Enterprise Metrics
- **Enterprise Pipeline**: Number of studios/OTTs in sales conversations
- **Pilot Success Rate**: % of pilots converting to paid subscriptions
- **Enterprise Churn**: % of enterprise accounts canceling
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: Revenue from existing customers over time
- **Average Contract Value (ACV)**: Average annual value of enterprise deals
