## YOUR VISION: HONEST ANALYSIS

### What You Got RIGHT

1. **No Social Media Poison** - Your decision to remove likes, comments, follow/unfollow, messaging is **BRILLIANT**. This is genuinely innovative. You're right that social media creates fake engagement, anxiety, and performative behavior.

2. **Privacy-First Approach** - Users can share opinions without fear of judgment. This is a **real differentiator**.

3. **B2B + B2C Model** - Selling insights to studios/OTT while collecting from audiences is a **legitimate business model** (similar to how Nielsen operates).

4. **The AI Data Vision** - Your idea that "if this data reaches every device, it can shape AI platforms and content creation" is **theoretically correct** but **years away from reality**.

### What You Got WRONG (Or Incomplete)

1. **Cold Start Problem** - You have 16 users. Studios won't pay for insights from 16 people. They won't even take a meeting.

2. **Chicken-and-Egg** - Users join for insights/rewards, but insights need users. You have neither right now.

3. **No Viral Loop** - Without social features, how do users discover you? You've removed the growth engine without replacing it.

4. **Coupon System** - You're giving coupons as rewards, but:
   - Coupons from aggregators have low conversion
   - Users don't care about random 10% off
   - This won't retain users

5. **Weekly Limit** - One opinion per category per week is too restrictive for engagement but too frequent for quality insights. It's in an awkward middle ground.
